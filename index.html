About PolicyPals

Founded in 2020 by a team of insurance experts and tech innovators, PolicyPals emerged from a simple observation: finding the right insurance shouldn't be complicated or confusing. Our founders experienced firsthand the frustration of navigating the insurance marketplace and decided to create a solution that would make insurance accessible and understandable for everyone.

Today, PolicyPals is driven by our mission to democratize insurance through technology and transparency. We believe in building trust through honest advice, clear communication, and continuous innovation. Our core values of accessibility, transparency, and community support guide everything we do, from developing our AI-powered matching system to providing 24/7 expert support.
